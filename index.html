The Environmental Challenge

📍 A San Gil Waste Problem

San Gil, the adventure capital of Colombia, is facing a critical waste management crisis. Our beautiful landscapes and the iconic Fonce River are being threatened by the lack of proper recycling culture and disposal infrastructure.

Every day, large amounts of mixed waste are produced, making it impossible to recover valuable materials. This not only pollutes our natural resources but also impacts the local tourism industry that San Gil depends on.

🚨 Critical Issues in San Gil:

  • River Contamination: Significant amounts of plastic waste end up in our water bodies.
  • Inefficient Separation: Most households do not separate organic from recyclable waste.
  • Regional Impact: The local landfill is under heavy pressure, reducing its operational lifespan.

Proposed Solutions

Educational Campaigns

We will develop digital workshops and interactive guides to teach citizens how to correctly classify waste (organic, plastic, glass, and paper) from their homes.

Adequate Labeling

Our project proposes a standardized visual system with clear colors and icons for recycling bins, ensuring that people can identify where to dispose of waste instantly.

Social Media Impact

We will use social media platforms to share daily tips and short videos, creating a massive digital movement that encourages sustainable living

Community Rewards

To motivate participation, we suggest an incentive system where active recyclers can receive discounts or benefits in local shops for their contributions.
